What is Project Flowerbed?

Project Flowerbed is an immersive, meditative VR gardening experience. Explore a tranquil island while you plant and grow flowers, trees, and more. Capture pictures of your garden as it comes to life, and share them with friends.

Project Flowerbed was created to celebrate what’s possible in WebXR. It is entirely open source, so other creators and developers around the world can take inspiration for their own WebXR projects.

We can’t wait to see what new ideas you bring to bloom!
