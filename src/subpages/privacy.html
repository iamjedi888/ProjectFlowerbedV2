Project Flowerbed Privacy Policy

The Project Flowerbed Experience is a web-based virtual reality (VR) experience that enables individuals to create a customized VR garden.

You may create a garden and save it locally on your device without creating a Meta account. When you interact with the site, we collect technical information about your browser or device, including through the use of cookies; we use that information to provide the experience and for analytics purposes to see how individuals interact on our website. The collection of this technical information is governed by the Meta Privacy Policy.
